Our Story

A Journey of Craftsmanship, Passion, and South African Heritage

Where It All Began

KgoTsheKhu was founded in 2018 with a simple mission: to preserve the art of traditional woodworking while creating beautiful, functional pieces for modern homes. Our name, derived from Setswana, means "to build" or "to create," reflecting our commitment to craftsmanship and creativity.

What started as a small workshop in Johannesburg has grown into a thriving business that celebrates South African artistry and sustainable practices. Every piece we create carries the spirit of our heritage and the dedication of our skilled artisans.

We believe that handcrafted items bring a unique warmth and character to any space. In an age of mass production, we stand firm in our commitment to quality, authenticity, and the timeless beauty of natural wood.

Our Craft Process

From raw wood to finished masterpiece

01

Material Selection

We carefully select premium quality wood from sustainable sources, ensuring each piece has beautiful grain and character.

02

Design & Planning

Our artisans sketch and plan each piece, considering functionality, aesthetics, and the natural properties of the wood.

03

Handcrafting

Using traditional techniques and modern tools, we shape, sand, and assemble each piece with precision and care.

04

Finishing Touches

We apply protective finishes, add custom engravings, and perform final quality checks before packaging.
