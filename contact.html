Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days within South Africa. Express shipping is available for 2-3 business day delivery.

Do you offer custom engraving?

Yes! We offer personalized engraving on most of our products. Simply select the custom text option when ordering.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for non-customized items. Custom engraved items can only be returned if defective.

How do I care for wooden products?

Wipe clean with a damp cloth. Do not soak in water. For cutting boards, apply mineral oil periodically to maintain the finish.

Do you ship internationally?

Currently, we only ship within South Africa. International shipping is coming soon!

Can I place a bulk order?

Absolutely! For bulk orders or corporate gifts, please contact us directly at orders@kgotshekhu.co.za for special pricing.
